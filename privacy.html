Privacy Policy

Fitkhao values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Data you provide such as name, profile picture, mobile number (for login), SMS reading permission for auto-fetching OTP, address, pincode, age, weight, height, and health-related inputs.
  • Location Data: Collected to determine if your area is serviceable.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To calculate BMI, BMR, and provide personalized nutrition recommendations.
  • To tailor calorie, protein, carbohydrate, and fat intake targets according to your body goals.
  • To determine service availability in your location.
  • To improve app functionality and user experience.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • With our clinical nutrition team for consultancy purposes.
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal and operational requirements.

6. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated through the app or our website.

7. Contact Us

For questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please visit www.fitkhao.com.
